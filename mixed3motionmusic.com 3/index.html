THE SOUND OF SURVIVING YOURSELF.

MIXED3MOTIONZ is the sound of a mind at war with itself trying to make peace through music. Built from late nights, bad decisions, family pressure, relationship scars, ADHD chaos, loyalty, regret, and the pressure to become more than the situation you came from.
