What We Collect

This site may collect your email address, optional phone number, contact-form details, and anything you choose to submit to The Wall. Do not submit private information you do not want reviewed by the site owner or posted publicly.

Submissions may be used to send music updates, merch drop access, booking replies, support messages, and moderated community posts. Phone numbers are optional and should only be used for SMS updates if SMS is enabled.

The Wall

Wall submissions are moderated before public display. The site owner may reject, edit, or remove posts that include private information, harassment, spam, threats, exploitation, or unsafe content.

Third-Party Services

The launch version is prepared for Netlify hosting and Netlify Forms. Streaming, social, merch, analytics, payment, email, or SMS tools may have their own privacy policies once connected.

Contact

Use the contact form on the website for privacy, booking, merch, or community questions. A public contact email can be added here later if MIXED3MOTIONZ chooses to publish one.
